PROJECT 1

Recording my Journey as
Type 2 Diabetic

This is a personal log of my average glucose level and combating Type 2 Diabetic by strict dieting (zero carb) and daily exercise.
By utilizing Google Forms to input different points of data entry, store it in Google Sheets and then feed to to Tableau, I'm able to create a live data viz of my health and keep tabs on it at all times.
