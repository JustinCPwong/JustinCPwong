Recording my Journey as
Type 2 Diabetic

This is a personal log of my average glucose level and combating Type 2 Diabetic by strict dieting (zero carb) and daily exercise.
Data log is done by pricking my finger twice a day to a snapshot of my blood glucose, once in the morning and another at night. The data points are entered through Google Forms and stored in Google Sheets. The dataset in Google Sheet is then fetched by Tableau Public to create the data viz.
