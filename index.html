An analyst with three years of practical experience in the analytical space. Experienced with multiple tools such as SQL, Excel, Tableau, Salesforce, and PowerPoint. Most of my experience comes from hands on where I am tasked to hunt down answers to business questions, solving real world business needs from stakeholders. Stakeholders ranges from executives at CEO/COO level, director of operations, and peers from other department such as the data team (data engineering). My belief in data is while it informs better decisions, but to drive action with data, one must create a continuous pipeline of questions about the next steps. In other words, do not stop at informed decision but constantly seeking “the next” pivot

During my free time - I like to dive into the trove of public data and explore the different dataset. Recently, I'm diving into CDC's NHPA data surrounding health.
